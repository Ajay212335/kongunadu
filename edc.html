Entrepreneurship Development Cell


Introduction

The Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC) was started with the object of promoting specialized knowledge in the field of entrepreneurship development. In view of the worldwide shortage of jobs (both government and private sectors) leading to unemployment problems and lack of proper utilization of human resources; the cell strives to identify talented youth to entrepreneurial works. The cell plans to organize various programmes regarding entrepreneurship development.

Objectives of the cell


(a) To create awareness among the students of the Institution regarding entrepreneurship as a career option.
(b) To conduct training programmes in the field of entrepreneurial skill development
(c) To develop entrepreneurship related course curriculum to incorporate in engineering programmes
(d) To provide guidance and facilities to first time entrepreneurs during gestation.
