Cisco Networking Academy


Cisco is the worldwide leader in IT that helps companies seize the opportunities of tomorrow by proving that amazing things can happen when you connect to the internet. This tradition process continues with industry leading products in the core areas of routing and switching, as well as advanced technologies in the areas such as Unified Communications, Network Security, Video, Virtualization and Cloud Computing.
To make the students know about the basics of networking and its essentials, this CISCO certification course has been started in our institution. The Faculty members got trained with Cisco sponsored academy and have been appointed as a trainee.

Certification Courses Included In Cisco

CCNA: (Cisco Certified Network Associate)

Module 1: Introduction to networks
Module 2: Routing and Switching Essentials
Module 3: Scaling Networks
Module 4: Connecting Networks

Emerging Trend Courses In Cisco:

CCNP (Cisco Certified Network Professional)
CCNP- Cloud, Collaboration, Security, Service Provider, Wireless and IoT
Through this certification course we train the students to learn the techniques used in networks and how to configure switch and router using simulation with different scenario. Highly technological devices are given to students during the course to get practice with real devices before completing the course.
Graduates from various disciplines like Electronics and Communication Engineering, Computer Science Engineering and Information Technology are getting trained through trainees.
