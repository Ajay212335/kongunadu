Women Development Cell


Women Development Cell was established in the college during 2012-13.

MOTTO: "Educating Women is Educating Generation"

Aim

The cell aims to empower girl students, to enhance understanding of issues related to women and to make the college campus a safe place for women students.

Objectives

1.To create awareness among the girls about the society
2.To promote a culture of respect and equality for female gender.
3.To remove the inferiority, shy and fear
4.To impart the knowledge of various subjects
5.To prevent any kind of women harassment in the campus
6.To motivate to became an Entrepreneur

1.Seminars/Workshop/Conference
